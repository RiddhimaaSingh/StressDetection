About Stress

Stress is a physical and emotional response to a situation that is perceived as challenging or demanding.While stress can be motivating and help us perform better under pressure, too much stress can have negative effects on our mental and physical health. It can lead to feelings of anxiety, irritability, fatigue, and even depression. Therefore, it is essential to manage stress effectively to prevent it from overwhelming us.

Know a little more About Me

Acute Stress

Stress which is a type of short-term stress that is caused by a specific event or situation. This type of stress can be intense, but it typically goes away once the situation is resolved.

Chronic Stress

Stress which is a type of long-term stress that occurs over an extended period, often weeks or months. It can be caused by ongoing work or relationship problems, financial difficulties, or health issues.

Mental Stress

Stress which refers to the emotional and cognitive responses to a stressful situation. This type of stress can cause individuals to feel overwhelmed, anxious, and unable to concentrate.

Behavioral Stress

Stress which refers to the actions and behaviors that result from stress. This can include engaging in unhealthy coping mechanisms, such as overeating, substance abuse, or avoidance

Emotional Stress

Stress which refers to the feelings and emotions that arise from a stressful situation. This can include feelings of anger, frustration, sadness, or anxiety
